About the author

Jesse Alexander is a multi-genre MM romance author with a flair for the fantastical and a deep love of emotional, character-driven stories. From regency ballrooms to enchanted cafés, from starships to smoky taverns, Jesse crafts stories where heart and heat go hand in hand — and love always finds a way. With a voice that's warm, witty, and just a little wicked, she invites readers into worlds where magic is real, passion runs deep, and happily-ever-afters come with a twist of the unexpected.

When not writing, Jesse enjoys dreaming up new adventures, playing with her lovely cats (who often sneak their way into her stories), and finding the perfect balance of angst, affection, and a bit of sparkle.
