The Duke's Christmas Consort

The plan was simple. Hire a fake consort, survive grandmama's Christmas, stay a bachelor. Tristan Quinnell was not part of the plan.

Michael St. James is not prepared to give up bachelorhood. However, his beloved ailing grandmama's demand that he settle down with a bride, or given his preference for men, with a permanent consort, weighs heavily on him. Resolved to make her happy and yet to escape the marriage trap, he resorts to hiring a man in good standing to play the part of his intended consort during a week of Christmastime festivities hosted by his grandmother.

Little does he realize that his "intended," Tristan Quinnell, comes with a few surprises that might just turn his ordered world topsy-turvy.

A male/male holiday story set in a fantasy world inspired by the regency period — delightfully funny, sweet, and at times steamy.
