Hot Lemonade series · Book 2

Forbidden Fruit

Stepbrothers. Fated mates. Starving for each other. Some fruit is forbidden for a reason.

Cain and Ash became stepbrothers as teenagers, and the pull between the two young incubi was never exactly brotherly. When their secret came to light, shame and family expectations tore them apart.

A decade later, a chance encounter at Hot Lemonade, the Quarter's most exclusive supernatural hookup spot, puts them face to face with everything they buried. In a place where consent is magical law and judgment is checked at the door, there's nowhere left to hide from what they've always wanted. Each other.

A high-heat MM paranormal romance of forbidden fated mates and second chances.
