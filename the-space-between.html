Hot Lemonade series · Book 1

The Space Between

They can't touch without risking destruction. So they find new ways to burn.

Fire elemental Aiden is chaos in motion. Uncontrolled, unpredictable, and hotter than sin. But when a frost elemental turns a block of ice into a dirty masterpiece, Aiden is wrecked. Hooked. Desperate to feel that kind of cold precision and hunger turned on him.

Miles has spent centuries in perfect control, his emotions locked behind ice-thick walls. Until one dangerously beautiful man crashes into his world like a wildfire. Now all Miles can think about is melting.

Their powers are opposites. Their bodies? Off-limits. Their connection, forbidden. But the craving? It's unbearable. So they get creative.

Magic barriers. Proxies. Illusions that chase them to the edge of pain and pleasure without crossing the fatal line. Every near-touch is agony. Every almost is addictive. But not touching hurts more than the risk ever could.

In the space between fire and frost, they invent a new kind of intimacy... and a love too explosive to be contained.

A sizzling MM paranormal romance where forbidden love ignites dangerous new possibilities.
