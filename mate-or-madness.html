The Mate or Madness series

Standalone MM romances. Fated mates, high heat, and a happily ever after every time.

In a world that wants them dead, a half-blood's only salvation is the one person fate built for them. Their anchor.

Half-bloods are hunted, feared, and running out of time. Some are executed on sight. Some are watched until they break. Some are dragged to the prison dimension of Bedlam. The rules change from one kind to the next. The ending doesn't, unless a fated mate gets there first.

An anchor can steady a breaking mind, hold the madness at bay, and give the unwanted somewhere they finally belong. But claiming that bond means defying everyone who wants them gone, and risking everything for a love the world calls forbidden.

Every book follows a new couple and a new corner of this world, with the same promise. Two men, one bond, and a love worth fighting for.
